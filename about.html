Who We Are

THE COMPANY
BEHIND THE BOLT

XPARQ Holdings Corp. is a multi-division technology and creative company that refuses to pick a lane. Founded in 2024, we set out to build an ecosystem where communication platforms, AI tools, gaming experiences, commerce infrastructure, and a music label all coexist under one roof — powered by the same bold, independent DNA.

We believe the future belongs to builders who can move across verticals without losing identity. Every XPARQ product carries the same DNA: bold, functional, and unapologetically original. We're not backed by legacy capital or beholden to convention. We build because we see gaps — and we fill them with ambition.
